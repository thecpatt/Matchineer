What is Matchineer?

Matchineer is a service for all students at App State to develop connections with each other. It works by having users fill out a form with basic personal information, your passions or interests, and a short personality test. This data will then be used to compare users with one another, finding similarities to pair form applicants. You will then be given your match results on the summary page (make sure to record these before leaving the page). Whether you are a freshman or a graduate student, Matchineer can work for you!

How to contact Matchineer

This website is designed to provide an interactive and user-friendly experience for filling out forms. If you find an issue on the website or would like to report a user, contact Carson Patterson at pattersoncw@appstate.edu. In the case you are reporting a user, make sure you provide documentation of any problems that are present.

Getting rid of your account

We understand that a user may want their account removed for one reason or another. We have a system in place that profiles created over six months ago will be deleted from the database. If you would like your profile to be removed sooner, contact Carson Patterson at pattersoncw@appstate.edu.
